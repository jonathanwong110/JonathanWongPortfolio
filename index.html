ABOUT



Hello! I'm Jonathan, a software engineer based in New York City.

I enjoy creating things that live on the internet, ranging from websites, applications, or anything in between. My goal is to always build products that provide the best experience possible.

Before coding, I studied Economics and Public Policy at Hunter College and worked in the political/non-profit sphere. After I discovered coding, I studied it at Flatiron School and graduated.

In my free time, I build small apps to learn new skills and write blog posts on anything and everything coding related on Medium. In addition to coding and programming, you'll find me learning, reading, sketching, or taking pictures with my DSLR camera.
