ABOUT

Hello! I'm Jonathan, a software engineer based in New York City.

I enjoy creating things that live on the internet, ranging from websites, applications, and every thing in between. My goal is to always build products that provide the best experience possible.

Before coding, I studied Economics and Public Policy at Hunter College (BA '17) and worked in government and non-profit. After I discovered coding, I studied it at Flatiron School and graduated.
